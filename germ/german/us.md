1. Umlauts (Ä, Ö, Ü):
Ä: Press Shift + " (double quotation mark) followed by A.
Ö: Press Shift + " followed by O.
Ü: Press Shift + " followed by U.

2. Eszett (ß):
ß: Press Right Alt (or AltGr) + S.

3. Other German Letters:
Ä: Shift + " + A
Ö: Shift + " + O
Ü: Shift + " + U
ß: Right Alt (or AltGr) + S
