1. Umlauts (Ă„, Ă–, Ăś):
Ă„: Press Shift + " (double quotation mark) followed by A.
Ă–: Press Shift + " followed by O.
Ăś: Press Shift + " followed by U.

2. Eszett (Ăź):
Ăź: Press Right Alt (or AltGr) + S.

3. Other German Letters:
Ă„: Shift + " + A
Ă–: Shift + " + O
Ăś: Shift + " + U
Ăź: Right Alt (or AltGr) + S
